Site Map

 

Master Page

Purpose

The master page’s purpose is to provide navigation throughout the website. Additionally the page aids the user in consistently understanding that they are on the same website as they navigate from page to page.

Data Collected

None

Data Displayed:

The company logo

Navigation

·        Home / Logo

·        My Account / Login

·        About

·        Contact

·        Shop

·        Cart

My Account will be displayed if the user is logged in, Login will appear if the user has not logged in. Home is used interchangeably with “Default.aspx” and is the hypertext reference location on click of the logo.

Page Design

 

 

 

 


Default

Purpose

This is the default landing page of customers when they visit the Team Net folder. This is the face of the website.

Data Collected

No data collected.

Data Displayed

Here will be displayed featured items and sales. These will be displayed in the form of a slider. Below this slider will be a series of three reviews of Case Relief’s services their names, title of reviews, and reviews themselves will be displayed.

Shop

Purpose

This is the page where the customer’s shopping experience will take place. The user will have the ability to add products into their cart for ordering from this page as well as simply browse items. On click of the photo or the name of a product, a modal window will appear and offer more details to the customer.

Data Collected

Items the customer wishes to order

Quantity of items to order

 Filtering information

Data Displayed

Product

·        Name

·        Photos

·        Details

·        Price

·        Rating

Cart

Purpose

Visiting the cart is first step in ordering the products selected. Here the customer will confirm the items in their cart and their quantities are correct. If they are not, they will have the option of changing the quantities and items in their cart.

Information Collected

Products and quantities to be finalized will be collected.

Information Displayed

Products in basket

·        Picture

·        Quantity

·        Name

·        Description

Confirm Shipping and Billing Information

Purpose

Here, the customer will provide their shipping and billing information. If they have an account and are logged in, their information will automatically populate the boxes for shipping and they will only need to fill in billing information. If they are not logged in, they will be directed to either login, Register, or fill out the information manually.

Information Collected

Customer billing / shipping

·        Address

·        Zip

·        State

·        Preferred billing method

·        City

·        Whether billing address is the same as shipping address

Billing Info

Purpose

This page collects billing information so that the customer can pay Case Relief money for their products. This is the final page before the order is finalized.

Data Collected

·        Security number

·        Expiration month

·        Expiration year

·        Name on card
